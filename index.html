About Me

I am a civil engineering student focused on understanding how structures are planned, analyzed and executed. I am building my skills in BIM and structural analysis to strengthen my ability to interpret how materials, loads and design decisions influence real project performance. I have working knowledge in building planning, quantity surveying and construction documentation. Exterior and interior design serve as supporting skills that help me improve spatial understanding, while graphic design allows me to communicate concepts with clarity. My goal is to develop strong technical capability and become a well-rounded civil engineer with practical design sense and versatility across planning, analysis and visualization.
